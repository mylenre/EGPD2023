Submit your abstract and enjoy a unique opportunity!

The European Geothermal PhD Days (EGPD) brings together Geothermal-related PhD students from all over Europe every year.

EGPD will offer the opportunity for PhD students to present their work in poster sessions. Moreover, participants will have the opportunity to visit the UK Geoenergy observatory in Glasgow, a research facility designed for investigating shallow, low-temperature, mine-water heat energy and potential heat storage resources. A guided tour will allow you to learn more about the project and the geothermal potential in the UK ground.

We are excited about your contribution and looking forward to welcoming you to Glasgow soon!

Submission guidelines

All participants must submit an abstract and are expected to present their work in poster sessions during the conference. Registration is FREE of charge! For submitting and abstract please follow these guidelines:

  • Include the title of your poster, authors, and author's affiliations.
  • The body of your abstract should not exceed 200 words.
  • Include a list of max. 5 keywords.
  • Indicate the general theme of your research

    • If you have any questions regarding abstract submission please contact us at egpd2023@gmail.com

    Poster sessions

    Please select one of the following topics and mention it in your submission.

    1. Deep Geothermal

    2. District Heating and Cooling

    3. Drilling Technology

    4. Enhanced Geothermal Systems and Induced Seismicity

    5. Exploration

    6. Fluids

    7. Policy and Markets

    8. Shallow Geothermal

    9. Surface and Monitoring Technology

    10. Thermal Energy Storage

    Any other topics related to geothermal, not mentioned in our list, are welcome and will be fit in the program where it makes the most sense.
