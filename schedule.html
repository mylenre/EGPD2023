Highlights of the event!

We are very happy to announce our great keynote speakers: Magdalena Scheck-Wenderoth (GFZ - Potsdam), and Marit Brommer (IGA).

Additionally we will have a site visit to Weiseiller. Here, an exploratory drilling phase from the premises of the Weisweiler power plant is scheduled for the second half of the year. This geothermal power plant will play a key role in reducing the carbon emissions of Germany.
