Sponsor Opportunities

Between 50 to 80 passionate Geothermal PhD students from around the globe will gather at the EGPD to connect with colleagues, learn about new developments, and study new ways of making the energy transition possible.

EGPD is your opportunity to advertise your company to the future leaders in the geothermal energy industry. It is also a great opportunity to show your company's investment in the energy transition and showcase your latest products and solutions.

Sponsorship Options

The following is a list of sponsorship opportunities that help make this a successful and pleasant conference. Please consider donating towards one of these financial expenditures of the event. In return, we will include your business on our website as a valued sponsor with the respective tier/level and acknowledge your contribution during the event week.

  • Coffee and Tea Breaks
  • Lunch and Conference Dinner
  • Food and Beverages for Social/Networking events
  • Travel to UKGEOS site
  • Funding student travel
  • Prize money for best poster/presentation
