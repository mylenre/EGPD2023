Sponsor Oportunities

Between 70 to 100 passionate Geothermal PhD students from around the globe will gather at the EGPD to connect with colleagues, enhance their knowledge, learn about new developements, and study new ways of making the energy transition possible.

EGPD is your opportunity to advertise your company to the future leaders in the geothermal energy industry. Aditionally you will have the oportunity to contact experts interested in your buisness, gain customers, and to increase the visibility of your brand, product, or service amongst the future of the Geothermal industry. It is also a great oportunity to show the investment that your company is making on the energy transition and to showcase your latest products and solutions.
