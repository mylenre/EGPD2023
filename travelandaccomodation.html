Location

We are proud to announce that the European Geothermal PhD Days (EGPD) 2023 will be hosted in two of the collaborating universities: the University of Glasgow and University of Strathclyde, both located in Glasgow.

The two venues are located at a walking distance of 40 min, 15 min using the subway and about 20 min by bus.

For more information on travel activities and how to plan your trip to Glasgow visit: Glasgow Tourism
